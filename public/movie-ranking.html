The Best Movies According to Me

Here are the top 10 movies that I have watched and enjoyed the most:

1. Tenet (2020)

- A mind-bending sci-fi thriller directed by Christopher Nolan that explores time manipulation and espionage.

2. Inception (2010)

- A mind-bending thriller directed by Christopher Nolan that explores the world of dreams and reality.

3. The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

- A powerful drama about hope and friendship set in a prison, based on a novella by Stephen King.

4. The Godfather (1972)

- A classic crime film directed by Francis Ford Coppola, depicting the rise and fall of a powerful mafia family.

5. Pulp Fiction (1994)

- Quentin Tarantino's iconic film that weaves together multiple stories of crime and redemption in Los Angeles.

6. The Dark Knight (2008)

- Another masterpiece by Christopher Nolan, featuring Batman's battle against the Joker in Gotham City.

7. Forrest Gump (1994)

- A heartwarming tale of an extraordinary individual who experiences significant historical events.

8. Marty Supreme (2025)

- A 2025 American comedy-drama film directed by Josh safdie

9. Interstellar (2014)

- A visually stunning sci-fi epic directed by Christopher Nolan that explores space travel and the survival of humanity.

10. The Matrix (1999)

- A groundbreaking sci-fi action film directed by the Wachowskis that delves into the nature of reality and human consciousness.
